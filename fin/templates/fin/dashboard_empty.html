Welcome to the UASFin Study!

To participate in this study, we are asking you to link your financial accounts to our secure website. This is done through Plaid, a company trusted by millions of users to help them securely manage their spending and saving.

Your financial information will be kept secure and confidential. Plaid will never share your credentials (usernames and passwords for your financial institutions), with UAS researchers or anyone else. Remember, UAS will NOT have access to your credentials or any identifying information (except for what we keep on file to communicate with you, such as name, address and phone number).

As with all survey and research data, UAS will never store your personal identifiers or contact information with your financial data, nor will we share that information with researchers, or anyone else.

For example, if you add accounts from five of your institutions, you will earn ${{ treatment.reward_link|mul:5 }} today, and ${{ treatment.reward_monthly|mul:5 }} for every month your institutions remain linked.
